The Current Saga

Whispering Steel: Echoes of the valley

In the quiet village of Zexin, ten-year-old Jian Xue stumbles upon a relic of unimaginable power — the Heaven's Fall Iron. Unaware of its significance, he awakens forces that will soon engulf his home in flames. By the next day, his village is reduced to ashes, and the young boy is forced to flee, carrying the legendary material that could forge the ultimate weapon. Journeying through the perilous Xiangjian Valley, Jian immerses himself in martial arts, forging the Whispering Steel sword with relentless determination. Along the way, he meets mentors who sharpen his skills, friends like Hu Xiao who stand by his side, and rivals who test the limits of his resolve. To acquire the rare materials for his sword, he braves deadly tournaments and ventures into the treacherous Baihua Forest, confronting dangers that would break even the bravest of warriors. Through trials, bloodshed, and the wisdom of masters, Jian transforms from a frightened boy into the valley’s champion. Mastering the secrets of the Whispering Steel, he leads his people against a tyrannical adversary and a nemesis who once hunted him. Victorious yet unfulfilled, Jian leaves the safety of his home, his heart burning with the desire for justice, as he sets out to confront the true architect of his village’s destruction. A tale of loss, vengeance, and the forging of destiny, Whispering steel:Echoes of the valley. marks the beginning of an epic saga where every blade tells a story, and every choice can ignite a war..

Whispering Steel: Rise of ash and steel

In the opulent yet treacherous courts of the Tianzhao Empire, young Prince Yu’s life shatters when a palace conspiracy claims the life of his mother. Stripped of his title and exiled from the capital, he vanishes from history, adopting a new identity as Lin Mu. Alone and hunted, he faces a world rife with treachery, betrayal, and deadly obstacles. Along his journey, Lin Mu finds loyalty in two steadfast companions, forming bonds that will shape his destiny. Through hardship, strategy, and sheer determination, he rises from an unknown exile to command a formidable army. Victories mount, and whispers of his might spread across the empire, earning him the title Heaven’s Scourge, Lord Tianhe. The path to power, however, is paved with trials. Lin Mu confronts the corrupt noble families who believe themselves above the law, navigates webs of intrigue, and uncovers allies and enemies in unexpected places. Amidst the chaos, he meets Jian Xue from Xiangjian Valley, whose mastery of the Whispering Steel becomes instrumental in the battles to come. Love, loyalty, and betrayal test Lin Mu at every turn as he carves his place in history. By the end, Lin Mu emerges as the most formidable figure in Tianzhao — a prince restored, a commander feared, and a force that restores order to a fractured empire. Whispering Steel: Rise of ash and steel, is a saga of ambition, vengeance, and the rise of a legend whose blade shapes the fate of nations.

Upcoming in the Saga

Whispering Steel: Queen of Crimson Sand

Tells the origin story of Tang Rong, from slave to warrior to Bandit Queen of Scarlet Blade Hall. Her life in the Tianlu Desert, battles with Suhuang clans, and rise to power lead her to an encounter with Prince Yu’s growing cause.

Whispering Steel: Shadows over Xueyang

Ye Kai, an elite assassin of Dark River, navigates a civil fracture within the guild and its war against General Fu Hong of the cold, misted Xueyang State. Slowly, the trail of the benefactor emerges again. At the end, Ye Kai meets Lord Tianhe and Jian — their fates begin to align.

Whispering Steel: The Fivefold Path

Yu, Jian, Ye Kai, Fu Hong, and Tang Rong converge — not always peacefully — in pursuit of the benefactor’s hidden influence. They travel from misty cliffs of Xueyang to the burning trails of Suhuang, discovering the magnitude of the enemy they face. Seeds of trust are tested.

Whispering Steel: The Severing Sky

The grand finale. The combined force marches to strike the benefactor's network across Tianyuan. They are joined by Xiao Daoren, a reclusive Daoist with roots in forgotten arts. The story culminates in a final confrontation at Yunhai Cliffs, where the benefactor began his rise.
