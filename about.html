About Me

Hello! I’m Stanley Rodney Nyakundi, a multidisciplinary creator and solutions-driven thinker. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Civil and Structural Engineering from the University of Eldoret, Kenya, where I built a solid foundation in design, analysis, and problem-solving through real-world engineering challenges.

In addition to my engineering background, I have developed strong capabilities in front-end web development, with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, allowing me to create responsive, user-centered web applications. My design sensibilities are further enhanced through my understanding of UI/UX principles and tools like Figma.

I am also an avid practitioner of AI tool integration, leveraging platforms such as ChatGPT, DeepSeek, and other generative AI systems for productivity, automation, research, and creative problem-solving. These technologies allow me to bridge complex ideas with innovative digital solutions.

While I maintain a keen interest in the financial world and occasionally explore currency trading, my true focus lies in combining technical knowledge with creativity to build impactful systems and experiences that align with modern digital evolution.
